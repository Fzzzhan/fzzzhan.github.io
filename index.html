Fengze Han 韩丰泽

Biography

Fengze Han (韩丰泽) is the Perception Lead at Altos Radar (Silicon Valley-based 4D imaging radar startup), where he leads the development of advanced perception algorithms for automotive radar systems and multi-modal sensor fusion architectures for autonomous vehicles. He received his M.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the Technical University of Munich (TUM), Germany, with specialization in robotics and machine perception. His prior industrial research experience includes positions as Senior R&D Engineer at Baidu Apollo and Alibaba Group, where he contributed to the development of SLAM algorithms and high-precision localization systems for autonomous navigation platforms. Originally from Zibo, Shandong Province (山东淄博), Mr. Han actively contributes to academic research through collaborations with international research groups and maintains involvement in open-source robotics communities.
